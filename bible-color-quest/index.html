Activities

Creative Bible play in one calm app

Color Bible scenes

Tap-to-fill regions, a crayon tool, eraser, undo, autosave, and parent-gated export for finished artwork.

Solve gentle puzzles

Preschool-friendly jigsaw puzzles use generous snapping so young children can play without precision frustration.

Play memory match

Simple icon cards and friendly labels create a quiet matching activity for short play sessions.

Scenes

Old Testament and New Testament moments

Bible Color Quest includes original Bible-inspired scenes such as Creation, Noah's Ark, David and Goliath, Jonah, the Birth of Jesus, the Good Samaritan, the Resurrection, and more.
