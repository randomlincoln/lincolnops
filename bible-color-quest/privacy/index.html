Privacy

Privacy Policy

Effective May 7, 2026

No account or advertising

Bible Color Quest does not require an account and does not include third-party advertising.

No analytics or tracking

The app does not include third-party analytics SDKs and does not ask to track users across apps or websites.

Local app data

Coloring progress and saved activity state are stored locally on the device so children can continue their work later.

Photos permission

If a parent exports a finished coloring page, iOS may request permission to add that image to the device Photos library.

Purchases

The full-version unlock uses Apple's in-app purchase system. Purchase processing is handled by Apple.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to support@lincolnops.com.
