Support

Help with Bible Color Quest

For app support, purchase restore questions, or parent settings help, email support@lincolnops.com.

Purchases

Use Restore Purchase in the app settings if the full version was already purchased with the same Apple ID.

Saved colorings

Coloring progress is saved locally on the device. Deleting the app may remove locally saved artwork.

Exporting artwork

Finished coloring pages can be exported from parent-gated settings. iOS may ask for permission to add the image to Photos.
